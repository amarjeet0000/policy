3. Delivery Policy

  • Orders are typically processed and prepared for shipment within 24–48 hours of confirmation.
  • Delivery time varies based on your location and product availability. We will provide an estimated delivery date at checkout.
  • You will receive tracking updates via the app or SMS once your order is out for delivery.
  • While we work with reliable courier partners, delays due to external factors (e.g., weather, public holidays, transport strikes) are not our responsibility.
  • Our delivery partner will attempt delivery at your address. If you are unavailable, a second attempt may be made. After multiple failed attempts, the order may be canceled and returned.

4. Cancellation, Return, and Refund Policy

Cancellation Policy

  • Orders can be canceled directly from the app or by contacting support **before** the item has been shipped.
  • Once an order is marked as "Shipped," it can no longer be canceled.
  • A confirmation of your cancellation will be sent via the app or WhatsApp.

Return Policy

  • We accept returns for defective, damaged, or incorrect products within **2 days** of the delivery date.
  • To initiate a return, please contact our support team with your order ID and images of the product issue.
  • Products must be returned in an unused condition, in their original packaging, and with all original tags and accessories intact.
  • **Non-Returnable Items:** For hygiene and safety reasons, certain items such as food products, personal care items, and undergarments cannot be returned.

Refund Policy

  • Refunds are issued for successfully canceled orders, approved returns, or orders that failed during payment.
  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within **6–7 business days**.
  • Please note that any transaction fees or payment gateway charges applied during the original purchase may not be refundable.
  • For returns, partial or full refunds are processed only after our team has received and inspected the returned product. The final refund amount will be based on the condition of the item.

  • You must notify us immediately of any unauthorized use of your account or any other breach of security.
  • Sauda Online Shopping App reserves the right to terminate accounts, refuse service, or cancel orders at our discretion if we believe a user has violated these policies.
