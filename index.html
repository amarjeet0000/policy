Quick Sauda - Shipping Policy

Effective Date: 03 December 2025

1. Delivery Timeframes

  • Online Grocery: 2 – 12 hours (location-based, typically same-day delivery)
  • Online Food: 30 – 90 minutes (distance-based, restaurant preparation time included)
  • Pharmacy: 1 – 4 hours (based on prescription verification and availability)
  • General Shopping: 1 – 3 business days (standard delivery)
  • Express Delivery: Available for select products with additional charges

Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to factors beyond our control, including weather conditions, traffic, product availability, and seller preparation time. During peak seasons, festivals, or special promotions, delivery times may be longer. We will notify you of any significant delays and provide updated delivery estimates.

2. Shipping Charges

  • Free delivery for orders above ₹499 across most categories.
  • Standard delivery fee: ₹49 for orders below ₹499.
  • Express delivery may include additional charges ranging from ₹30 to ₹100 based on urgency.
  • Remote area surcharge may apply for locations outside our standard delivery zones.
  • Bulky item surcharge may apply for large or heavy products.
  • Shipping charges are clearly displayed during checkout before payment confirmation.

3. Delivery Areas

We currently deliver to select cities and regions in Bihar and neighboring areas. To check if we deliver to your area, please enter your pin code during checkout or on our website. We are continuously expanding our delivery network to serve more locations.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is confirmed and shipped, you will receive a confirmation email/SMS with tracking information and estimated delivery time. You can also track your order in real-time through your Quick Sauda account or via the tracking link provided.

5. Delivery Instructions

You can provide specific delivery instructions during checkout, such as leaving the package with a neighbor, security guard, or in a secure location. While we try to accommodate these requests, we cannot guarantee compliance in all cases for security reasons. For perishable items, we recommend being available to receive the delivery personally.

6. Failed Deliveries

If delivery attempts fail for the following reasons, additional charges may apply for redelivery:

  • Incorrect or incomplete address provided by customer
  • Recipient not available at the time of delivery
  • Insufficient access to the delivery location
  • Refusal to accept delivery without valid reason
  • Security restrictions preventing delivery

We typically attempt delivery twice. After three failed delivery attempts, the order will be returned to the seller and a refund will be processed minus any applicable restocking fees (typically 10-15% of order value).

7. Lost or Damaged Shipments

Report damaged products immediately upon receipt with photos. Claims will be investigated promptly, typically within 24-48 hours.

  • Inspect your package upon delivery before signing the delivery receipt.
  • Refuse delivery if the package appears tampered with or significantly damaged.
  • Report any issues within 48 hours of delivery for non-perishable items.
  • Report issues within 2 hours for perishable items like food and groceries.
  • Provide clear photographs of damaged items and packaging from multiple angles.
  • We will arrange for replacement, partial refund, or full refund as appropriate.
  • For lost shipments, contact us if your order hasn't arrived within 24 hours of the estimated delivery time.

8. Order Changes and Cancellations

You may modify or cancel your order within 15 minutes of placing it, provided the order hasn't been processed for shipping. Once an order is in the shipping process, changes cannot be guaranteed. For cancellations after the 15-minute window but before shipping, a cancellation fee of 5% may apply.

9. International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within India. We do not offer international shipping services.

10. Delivery During Special Circumstances

During festivals, extreme weather conditions, or local restrictions, delivery times may be extended. We will communicate any known delays through our platform, email, or SMS notifications. In case of government-imposed lockdowns or curfews, we will follow all applicable guidelines and may offer contactless delivery options.

  • Grocery: Report damaged, expired, or missing items within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Food: Report incorrect, spoiled, or quality issues within 2 hours of delivery.
  • Pharmacy: Non-prescription items may be returned within 7 days if unopened and in original packaging.
  • General Merchandise: Eligible for return within 7 days of delivery if unused and in original condition.
  • Electronics & Appliances: Manufacturer warranty applies; report defects within 10 days.
  • Farming Supplies: Eligible for return within 15 days if unused and in original packaging.

The following items are generally not eligible for return unless defective or incorrect:

  • Perishable groceries (fruits, vegetables, dairy, meat)
  • Prepared food and meals
  • Personal care items (if opened or used)
  • Prescription medications (unless dispensing error)
  • Items marked as 'non-returnable' or 'final sale'
  • Customized or personalized products
  • Innerwear and hosiery for hygiene reasons

2. Return Condition

Non-perishable items must be returned in original, unused condition with all tags, labels, and packaging intact for a full refund.

  • Items must be unused, undamaged, and in original saleable condition.
  • All original accessories, manuals, warranty cards, and freebies must be included.
  • Return shipping is free for eligible returns initiated through proper channels.
  • We reserve the right to reject returns that don't meet these conditions or show signs of use.
  • For damaged or defective items, photographs must be provided as evidence.

  • Initiate return request through your Quick Sauda account or contact customer service.
  • Provide reason for return and supporting evidence (photos/videos if applicable).
  • Once approved, we will schedule a pickup (typically within 24-48 hours) or provide return instructions.
  • After we receive and inspect the returned item (within 3-5 business days), we will process your refund.
  • You will receive email/SMS notifications at each stage of the return process.

  • Online Payments: Refunds processed within 7 business days of return approval.
  • COD Refunds: Processed to bank accounts within 10 – 15 business days.

  • For cash on delivery orders, refunds will be processed to your registered bank account.
